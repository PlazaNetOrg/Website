1. Introduction

Welcome to PlazaNet. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By accessing or using PlazaNet, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (hashed), date of birth (if provided, day and month only)
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, locale preference
  • Gaming Information: Currently playing game
  • Content You Create: Posts, comments, drawings, community contributions, likes, and interactions
  • Communication: Support requests, feedback, and bug reports

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Activity Data: Last seen timestamp, status changes, gaming activity
  • Log Data: Server logs for error tracking and security (not retained long-term)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Device information (OS version, device model, identifiers)
  • Browser information (User-Agent, browser type)
  • Usage analytics (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
  • Referrer information
  • Precise location data
  • Tracking pixels or third-party cookies

Note: We do not collect data through third-party cookies or tracking pixels. We only collect data necessary for providing our services.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Delivering all features and functionality
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Connecting you with friends and communities

3.2 Communication

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Sending service announcements
  • Notifying you of important updates
  • Security alerts and account notifications

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Fixing bugs and improving performance
  • Developing new features

3.4 Safety and Legal Compliance

  • Detecting abuse
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting rights and property

Legal Basis: We process your data based on: (1) your consent, (2) contract performance, (3) legal obligation, (4) legitimate interests, and (5) public task in the EU under GDPR.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 What We Don't Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT share data with advertisers
  • We do NOT share data with data brokers
  • We do NOT use your data for marketing purposes without consent

4.2 When We Do Share Data

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate (hosting provider)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To prevent abuse, or protect user safety
  • Public Content: Your profile and public posts are visible to other users
  • Data Transfer: In case of merger/acquisition, data may transfer (you'll be notified)

4.3 Public Profile Information

Your public profile (username, display name, avatar, bio, publicly shared content) is visible to other PlazaNet users and may be indexed by search engines. You control what information is public through your privacy settings.

4.4 Friends and Communities

Your friends can see your gaming activity, status, and publicly shared content based on your privacy settings. You can manage these settings at any time.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained indefinitely until account deletion
  • Game Activity: During playtime
  • Posts and Content: Retained indefinitely until deleted by user
  • Server Logs: Retained for 30 days

5.2 Account Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time through your account settings or by contacting us. When you delete your account:

  • Your account is immediately inaccessible
  • Your profile information is removed from view
  • Your personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Some aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics
  • Posts may remain visible if others have shared them (community content)

5.3 Data Export

You can request a copy of all your personal data in a portable format. This includes account information, posts, activity history, and preferences. Exports are provided within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right of Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right of Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right of Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Oppose processing for direct marketing or legitimate interests
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making: Not subject to automated decisions

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request to privacy@plazanet.org with:

  • Your account username
  • The specific right you're requesting
  • Proof of identity

We will respond within 30 days. You may also appoint an authorized representative to make requests on your behalf.

7. Security

7.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted via TLS/SSL encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data encrypted in our database
  • Password Security: Passwords hashed with bcrypt (not reversible)
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
  • Authentication: Two-factor authentication available

7.2 Security Incidents

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Investigate immediately
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Take steps to prevent future incidents

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password. Never share your password or recovery codes with anyone.

8. Cookies and Tracking

8.1 Our Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session Management: Keeping you logged in
  • Security: CSRF tokens and anti-fraud measures
  • Preferences: Remembering your language and theme settings

9. Children's Privacy

PlazaNet is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us at privacy@plazanet.org.

Children aged 13-18 may use PlazaNet with parental consent. We encourage parents to review this policy with their children.

10. Third-Party Applications and Self-Hosted Servers

10.1 Third-Party Applications

PlazaNet is an open-source project. Third-party developers may create applications that connect to our official servers. These applications:

  • Are NOT operated by us
  • May have different privacy practices
  • We do not vet or approve
  • Are the developer's responsibility

Important: Only authorize apps you trust. Review their privacy policies before granting access.

10.2 Self-Hosted Servers

PlazaNet's source code is available for self-hosting. If you run or connect to a self-hosted PlazaNet instance:

  • This Privacy Policy does NOT apply
  • The server operator is responsible for privacy
  • Contact the server operator about their privacy practices
  • We have no responsibility for their data handling
  • We do not monitor or audit self-hosted servers

Warning: Connecting to a self-hosted server means trusting that operator with your data. Verify they use HTTPS encryption and have a privacy policy you agree with.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Notify you via email if the changes are significant

Your continued use of PlazaNet after changes means you accept the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
