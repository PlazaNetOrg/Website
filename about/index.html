PlazaNet

Connect, share, and play — just like the good old Miiverse, but reimagined for today.

Welcome to PlazaNet!

PlazaNet is a friendly social network built on the belief that online communities should feel personal and creative!

Inspired by the golden age of Nintendo's Miiverse, PlazaNet offers a space to connect with friends and express yourself through unique drawing tools and Pal avatars.

What makes PlazaNet different?

Built by Community

PlazaNet is not only filled with content by the community, but also developed by it. Thanks to its open-source nature, anyone can add features and fix bugs.

A Focus on Connection

PlazaNet is structured around communities & specific topics, making it easy to find like-minded people, whether you're talking about your favorite game or sharing a doodle.

Creative Expression is Key

Forget boring text feeds. Every user can easily create hand-drawn posts and react to posts using "Woahs".

Ever wanted to own a plaza?

While PlazaNet has its official instance, the magic doesn’t stop there. Because it’s fully self-hostable, you can spin up your own server and create a private plaza just for friends, family, or any group you can imagine.

* PlazaNet is written in Go and works on any system that can run it, but development and official deployment happen on Linux.
